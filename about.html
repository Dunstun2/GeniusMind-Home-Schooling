📖 Our History

Genius Minds Homeschooling was founded in Nairobi, Kenya, out of a deep conviction that every child deserves personalised, high-quality academic support. Starting with a small team of dedicated educators, we grew rapidly through word-of-mouth as parents saw remarkable improvements in their children's grades and confidence. Today, we support students across multiple curricula — IGCSE, CBC, and 8-4-4 — through online, home-based, and physical centre sessions, having helped hundreds of families across Nairobi and beyond.

🧠 Teaching Philosophy

We believe that every child learns differently. Our approach is rooted in identifying each student's unique learning style and crafting personalised lesson plans that build strong foundations, encourage curiosity, and foster independent thinking. We combine structured academic rigour with warmth and encouragement, ensuring students not only pass exams but genuinely love learning.
