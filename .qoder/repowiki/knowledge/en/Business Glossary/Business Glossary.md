### IGCSE
- Definition：International General Certificate of Secondary Education curriculum offered by Genius Minds Homeschooling as one of its tutoring tracks.

### CBE/CBC
- Definition：Competency-Based Education / Competency-Based Curriculum — the Kenyan education system supported by the homeschooling service.
- Aliases：CBC

### 8-4-4
- Definition：The legacy Kenyan education structure (8 years primary, 4 years secondary, 4 years university) still listed among the curricula the service supports.

### Junior Programmer Bootcamp
- Definition：A promotional event/course advertised on the homepage highlights banners, covering Python, Scratch, and web design for children.
- Aliases：upcoming coder

### Booking Tracker
- Definition：The public-facing page (`public/track.html`) where clients look up their tutoring session bookings by a tracking key generated at booking time.
- Aliases：track page
